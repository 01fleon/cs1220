About me

My start at life took place on top of a mountain village. My parents and the people of my village raised me to surive by hunting not only on the mountains. But to the mountains themselves. Festing on mountain fleash has made by teeth and body strong.

While out surfing one day, I was struck by lightning and bit by a shark. This gave me the reflexes necessary to play on the U.S. Olympic Ping Pong team to win gold in 2016. It was then that I realised what direction I needed to take my life next.

Some say I seem older than I look. They are right. Mentally I am over a thousand years old. I have dreams that are worth 15 years of memory in one night of slumber. I stumbled acrossed this ability by being really tired one day; so tired that I fell asleep in my dreams, which lead to another dream and etc.

Don't try to find me.
